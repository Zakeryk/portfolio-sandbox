❖ What is FinCraft?

Defend Your Wealth

Assign budgets to towers. Stop expenses before they breach your walls and damage your net worth.

Air-Gapped Privacy

No bank logins. Your data lives locally on your device—offline and 100% safe from breaches.

Level Up Real Life

Evolve from a tent to a stone fortress as you save. Keep your net worth health bar full to win.

The Supply Drop

Drag and drop CSVs to reload resources. No broken APIs, just pure manual control.

Fight the Blight

Debt is corruption spreading across your map. Pay it down before it drains your gold mines.

The Battle Log

Treat transactions as enemy encounters. Defeat expenses to keep your base standing.
